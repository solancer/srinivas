Sindhi Directory 2016

Dear Friends,


Welcome to the Online Application Centre for the 2016 Sindhi Directory, brought about by the Sindhi Federation of Bangalore in association with Marching Ants!


Rolled out for the first time in 1970, the primary aim of the Directory has been to promote fellowship amongst the Sindhi Brethren and in doing so, the federation has been periodically rolling out a very useful Directory, updated with the latest addresses and other useful information. The information furnished in the Directory has then been used to facilitate regular & intimate communication about the community, such as its Cultural, Social, Religious & Incidental activities.


The last directory was published five years ago and the time has come once again, for it to be revised and updated. We've added a few more fields this time, based on feedback received and do believe that you will find it useful and non-intrusive. Also, in keeping with the Internet Age, we've partnered with Marching Ants, an agency headed by someone from our community to maintain an online version of the same as well.


We do sincerely hope you will see value in our efforts & join/update your contact information with us. Don't forget to please spread the word amongst our brethren, so we could include as many members as possible & build a strong and well-connected network.


Note: Your listing in the Directory is free, so please fill out the online form with all the relevant details requested and save it, so that the information could be used towards the forthcoming edition.


Thanking you.


Yours sincerely,
